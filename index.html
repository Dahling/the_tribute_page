Sally



This is my mom, Sally. She has the sweetest heart; and, she also happens to be extremely beautiful on the outside, as well. With her big brown eyes and long eyelashes, she will say the cutest, most random things that are so funny; she doesn't even do it on purpose.


Sally loves to have fun. When driving in the car, she'll be the first to sway back and forth, do a little dance move (i.e., the cutest dance move, ever), and sing every word to the song. She always gets super excited when particular songs come on; one of them is "Sugar" by Maroon Five. Decades ago, she would use the cigarette lighter as a microphone, which I especially enjoyed --that, and she would sometimes push the break pedal during the beats of a song, though that was a once-in-a-blue-moon thrill (special occasion).



Ever since I was little, I've been her #1 fan. She has taught me how, and what it means to love, unconditionally; giving unconditional love is one of the things she does best. This girl is all about love. In fact, when I was little, her favorite thing was a heart. During every occasion, she would ask for something (as long as it was in the shape of heart). She would also find hearts, everywhere. One time, she found a bagel that was shaped in a heart, and she encouraged me to save it; it sat on my desk for the longest time. She taught me this -to always look for hearts.


Sally is not only the greatest mom, but she is my best friend. During times I find myself feeling unlucky for various reasons in life, I always know that I am truly the luckiest person, when it comes to having her as my mom.
